Privacy Policy — Bageecha

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Bageecha ("we", "us", "our") operates the Bageecha mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Phone Number
We use Firebase Authentication to verify your identity via OTP (one-time password) sent to your phone number. Your phone number is stored on our servers and used solely to identify your account and send care reminders via WhatsApp, if you opt in. We do not use your phone number for marketing calls or share it with third parties for advertising purposes.

Name and City
If you provide your name and city during onboarding, we store this to personalize your experience (e.g. weather-based care tips for your city) and to display your name within the app.

Location
We use your city (or, if you grant permission, your device's GPS location) to fetch local weather data and provide watering/care recommendations suited to your climate. We do not store your precise GPS coordinates — only the city name is retained, and only for the purpose of weather lookups.

Plant Photos
When you use Plant Scan, Plant Doctor, or add a journal photo, you may upload photos of your plants. These photos are used to identify plant species, diagnose pests, diseases, and deficiencies, and illustrate your plant journal. We do not sell, share, or use your photos for any purpose other than providing the features you use them for. Photos are processed via third-party AI vision services for identification/diagnosis and are not retained by those services beyond the processing request, per their own data policies.

Usage Data
We collect data about how you use the app — plants added, tasks completed, chat messages sent to our AI assistant, and similar activity — in order to operate core features (reminders, streaks, badges, garden health scoring) and improve the app.

Payment Information
If you subscribe to Bageecha Premium, payment is processed by Razorpay. We do not store your card or payment details — Razorpay handles this in compliance with PCI-DSS standards. We retain only subscription status and transaction references.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the App's core features
  • Send care reminders (push notification and/or WhatsApp, if opted in)
  • Personalize weather and care advice
  • Diagnose plant health issues via AI
  • Process subscription payments
  • Improve the App based on aggregate usage patterns

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google) — for authentication (OTP verification)
  • AI service providers — to process plant photos for identification/diagnosis and to power the AI chat assistant
  • Razorpay — to process subscription payments
  • Weather data providers — to fetch weather for your city
  • Law enforcement or regulators, only if legally required

4. Data Retention & Account Deletion

You can request account deletion at any time from Profile → Delete Account. Once requested, your account enters a 30-day grace period, during which you can restore it by logging back in. After 30 days, your account and all associated data (plants, journal entries, chat history, doctor diagnoses, photos, badges) are permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems.

5. Your Rights

  • Access or update your profile information from within the app
  • Request deletion of your account and data (see above)
  • Opt out of WhatsApp notifications and push notifications from Settings

6. Children's Privacy

Bageecha is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure authentication. No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via the app or via the email/phone number on file.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at: support@bageecha.app

This policy applies to the Bageecha app available on the Google Play Store.
