Delete Your Account

Last updated: July 22, 2026

You can request deletion of your Bageecha account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to do it, and exactly what happens to your data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Bageecha app and log in
  2. Go to Profile (bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll to Account Actions
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion request
No app installed and want to request deletion anyway?
Email us at support@bageecha.app with the phone number linked to your account, and we'll process your deletion request manually within 7 business days.

What Happens After You Request Deletion

Your account enters a 30-day grace period:

  • You are immediately logged out of the app
  • Your data is not yet deleted — it's preserved in case you change your mind
  • If you log back in within 30 days, you'll be shown a "Restore your account?" screen. Tapping Restore cancels the deletion and everything continues exactly as before
  • If you do nothing for 30 days, your account and all associated data are permanently and irreversibly deleted

What Gets Deleted

Once the 30-day grace period ends, the following are permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your profile (name, phone number, city)
  • All plants in your garden and their care history
  • Journal entries and photos
  • Plant Doctor diagnosis history
  • AI Plant Chat conversation history
  • Badges, streaks, points, and level progress
  • Collections
  • Subscription and payment records (transaction references — no card details are ever stored by us)
  • Push notification tokens
This action is irreversible after the 30-day window. There is no way to recover your data once the grace period ends and deletion is processed.

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or data retention, contact us at support@bageecha.app. See our Privacy Policy for more on how we handle your data.

This page applies to the Bageecha app available on the Google Play Store.
